I plan to pursue careers involving in this major in college as well. Though I am a beginner, I am willing to learn more to expand and improve my skill set. I swim competitively ever since I was 8 years old. I won some and lost some. But overall, I had a lot of fun whether it be one or the other.

Brief Experience as a Swimmer

As I mentioned in the 'About Me' section, I started swimming when I was 8 years old. It would be a lie if I said that I loved swimming the moment I started. I was "forced" to do so since my 2 oldest sisters became swimmers as well. Due to that, I was expected to follow suit. But of course, like most of other events, I came to appreciate what my parents did. I was able to meet new life-long friends, and experience the joy of traveling, playing, training, and competing with them. We participated multiple regional competitions and marathons and won numerous medals. And Although there were times I felt down due to the result, they were there for me to support me back up.
